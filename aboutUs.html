About Us

We are a government-driven initiative ensuring efficient distribution of subsidized fertilizers to farmers across the country.

Our platform brings **transparency, accountability, and ease of access** for both farmers and agricultural officers.

By leveraging digital solutions, we ensure fertilizers reach the right farmers at the right time, minimizing inefficiencies and ensuring fairness.

Our Objectives

  • Ensure fair distribution of fertilizers
  • Track farmer eligibility and requests
  • Monitor stock and supply chain management
  • Streamline communication between officers and farmers
