About Us

a government-driven initiative aimed at ensuring the efficient distribution of subsidized fertilizers to farmers across the country.

Our platform is designed to streamline the process of fertilizer allocation,
bringing transparency, accountability, and ease of access for farmers and agricultural officers.

We believe in empowering farmers by providing them with the necessary resources
to enhance agricultural productivity and sustain food security. Our digital system ensures that fertilizers reach
the right farmers at the right time, eliminating inefficiencies and ensuring fairness in distribution.
With a multi-tiered officer structure, stock management, and real-time tracking,
we aim to make the fertilizer distribution system more reliable, accessible, and corruption-free.

This system is designed to help farmers receive government-subsidized fertilizers efficiently while ensuring transparency in distribution.

Our Objectives

  • Ensure fair distribution of fertilizers.
  • Track farmer eligibility and requests.
  • Monitor stock and supply chain management.
  • Streamline communication between officers and farmers.
